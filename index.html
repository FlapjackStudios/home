About Us

We are young, inexperienced developers striving to build something extraordinary. Starting with nothing but our passion and creativity, we founded Flapjack Studios to bring our ideas to life. Our journey is fueled by a desire to learn, create, and share our stories with the world.

Our Games

  • Broken Bullets: Arise (2015) - A former criminal is freed from prison to take down a terrorist organization.
  • Broken Bullets: Dynasty (2016) - Danny Torrez returns from *Arise* to hunt down the infamous Nepali Terrorists.
  • Broken Bullets: Unchained (2016) - During riots tied to an election, a prisoner escapes amid chaos, only to find the world unrecognizable after 10 years.
  • Broken Bullets: Enforced (2020) - After the U.S. president's assassination, a divided nation falls into turmoil. A police officer in NYC must restore order.
  • Broken Bullets: Resurgence (2013) - A former CIA operative on vacation stumbles upon secrets that lead to a government lockdown. Can he escape the island?
